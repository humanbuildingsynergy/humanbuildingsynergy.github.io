Join Us!

The HUBS Lab is looking for motivated and talented individuals to join our fresh and growing research team!

What we offer

The HUBS lab will be fully committed to your success!

You will become a knowledge HUB of

  • Smart and Sustainable Buildings,
  • Occupant Behavior in Buildings,
  • Building | Community | Urban Energy Modeling.

In so doing, let's

  • Build the vision for your career,
  • Set up milestones during your course,
  • Have weekly individual meetings with Dr. Jung,
  • Have monthly group meetings to learn from your peers,

You will be exposed to

  • Multidisciplinary research,
  • Data-driven approaches like machine & deep learning,
  • Electronics using sensors, networks,
  • Scientific thought processes,
  • And More!

What we expect

Prepared to become a HUB!

Specifically,

  • Be motivative and passionate!
  • Devoted yourself to research and be responsible for it,
  • Be curious about new knowledge and willing to learn them,
  • Be positive and interactive with peers.

in terms of knowledge, experience, skills

  • Background in building science,
  • Research experience with tangible outcomes (e.g., peer-reviewed journal or conference publications),
  • Excellent English verbal and written communication skills,
  • Programming experience (Python, R),
  • Electronics experience (e.g., Arduino, Raspberry Pi),
  • Building Energy Modeling experience.

Do not worry too much even if you don't have much experiences. We all start from scratch.

Ph.D. course

Image you becoming a faculty member after graduation!
 It seems like a long journey, taking more than three-four years. However, once you become aware of where you'd stand after graduation, it might not be long.
Please be aware of the fact that you will gain the privilege of applying for an assistant professor position! This position requires you to get funding, conduct your research, teach undergraduate/graduate courses, mentor graduate students, and serve your communities!
To become a strong candidate, it is highly recommended to publish more than three journal articles as the main contributor when you apply for a position.
The HUBS lab will guide you to accomplish your goals during this course.

Master's course

You are becoming a researcher!
 A researcher creates knowledge! Specifically, you understand the body of knowledge and scientifically create the necessary knowledge for peers or the public!
By being involved in research projects led by the HUBS lab, you will become not only a researcher, but also a HUB!
If interested in a Ph.D. degree, it is highly recommended to publish one journal article. This experience will prepare yourself to become a successful scholar!

How to apply?

Check out the CAEM graduate programs and apply now!
Don't forget to mention Dr. Wooyoung Jung in your personal statement so that your application will be brought to our attention!
