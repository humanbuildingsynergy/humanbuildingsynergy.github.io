Vision

The Human Building Synergy Laboratory (HUBS) envisions deeply synergistic interactions between humans and buildings through better context awareness and intelligent actions.
Specifically, HUBS dreams of benefiting individuals from the built environments in any context while efficiently and sustainably consuming energy.
